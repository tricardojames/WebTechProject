Dog of the Month

The dog of the month is the adorable frenchbulldog! Each month we will upload our choice for the dog of the month. As the winter season approaches there's no dog better to snuggle up with than the frenchbull dog. This small breed is perfect for those who live in apartments or those who lack the outside space that a larger breed will require. Despite being relatively low maintenance , this breed is full of character and is known to be an entertainer. Watch this cute video of french bull dogs!!
